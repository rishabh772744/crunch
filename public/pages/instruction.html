INSTRUCTIONS & T&C

Please read the following very carefully.

General:

  • The tournament is for PUBG Mobile and only mobile players are allowed. Emulators and any other devices apart from smartphones and tablets are strictly prohibited. In case if someone pays and registers himself/herself, and is later found to be using emulators, s/he will be disqualified for any prizes and his/her money WILL NOT be refunded.
  • No hacks/mods are allowed. Anyone caught will be disqualified for any prizes and their money WILL NOT be refunded.
  • If anyone pays/registers and is not able to come online before the match starts at the scheduled time, his/her money WILL NOT be refunded.
  • In case of Solo Classic Matches, the first 100 will be allowed to play for a match. No submission/payment will be allowed after 100 regitrations per match. In the rare case where someone has paid after the 100th registration, he/she will be automatically booked for a match in the next slot.
  • Teaming up in Solo Matches is stricly prohibited. Our team spectates each match very carefully. If anyone is caught, he/she will be disqualified for any prizes.
  • In the unfortunate case where your game crashes during the match, you have to immediately write to us on our email. If you send the email during the match time, our spectators will verify your gameplay and allow you to play the next match without any fees.
  • Prizes, Schedule & Entry Fees keep changing. It is advised that you follow our Instagram and Facebook Handle for daily updates. We would also update the same on the website.
  • You are requested to login to the room 10 minutes before the start of match/when you receive the ROOM ID & PASSWORD from us(whichever time is later).
  • Once you join the room, please do not close/minimize your app, since that would remove you from the room and you would have to login again. We will not be responsible if you are removed from the room because of you minimizing/closing the app.
  • For any details/enquires, please contact us at: people@crunched.in

Prizes & Prices:

  • There are two types of prizes: Superior & Premium. Superior Prizes are awarded after every match. Premium Prizes are awarded every month. More details about the same can be found on the Prizes page.
  • Entry fee of matches change from time to time. You are advised to follow our Instagram & Facebook pages for daily updates.
  • We can change the daily cash prize of the matches, if we do not recieve enough registrations, which is though very rare. If all 100 people are registered, prizes will be distributed from the entire prize money pool, as informed before.
  • In case of Free2Play Tournaments, only the people who have paid the entry fee would be eligible for daily prizes. All participants would be eligible for only monthly prizes.
